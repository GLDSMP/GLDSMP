Server Rules

Keep it fair, friendly, and fun for everyone!

  • No hacked clients / cheats

    Any cheating (x-ray, kill-aura, auto-miners, etc.) is strictly prohibited and will be punished.

  • No freecam / camera exploits

    Freecam mods or spectator exploits used to gain advantage are banned.

  • No toxic behaviour / harassment

    Be respectful. Hate speech, doxxing, harassment or targeted abuse will lead to immediate action.

  • Staff decisions are final

    Staff are responsible for keeping the server fair — their decisions are final. Appeal processes available on Discord.

  • Exploits & bugs

    If you find a bug or exploit, report it to staff. Abusing exploits may result in bans.

Penalties: warnings → temporary ban → permanent ban depending on severity.
