Server Rules

Keep it fair, friendly, and fun for everyone!

  • No hacked clients / cheats

    Any form of cheating, such as x-ray, kill aura, or auto-mining mods, is strictly forbidden.

  • No freecam / camera exploits

    Freecam mods or spectator exploits to spy or gain unfair advantages are not allowed.

  • No toxic behaviour / harassment

    Keep chat friendly and respectful. Racism, hate speech, and harassment result in instant bans.

  • Staff decisions are final

    Respect staff authority. Their word is final to keep the peace and fairness on the server.

  • Exploits & bugs

    Don’t abuse game bugs. Report them to staff immediately.
